How I Started CS

I had gotten curious about computers and software as a high school student. It seemed like magic that time to see games like Minecraft just launch and work! I got curious over time. Thus, I took my first introductory computer science class in high school. As my passion for the subject grew, I became interested in tinkering with programming in Python.

After high school, the decision to major in computer science while I studied in a community college came. At least two and a half years passed. During that time, I took multiple courses to learn about topics: introduction to programming languages, understanding how algorithms work, and other topics such as advanced Java programming with databases. The major became my passion worth pursuing. To this day, I brainstorm, create, and tinker.
